OF FINELY CHOPPED, MINCED MEATS DE MINUT ALIBUS [1]

[164] A MINCE OF SEA FOOD MINUTAL MARINUM

PLACE THE FISH IN SAUCE PAN, ADD BROTH OIL AND WINE [and poach it]. ALSO FINELY CHOP LEEK HEADS [the white part only of leeks] AND [fresh] CORIANDER.
