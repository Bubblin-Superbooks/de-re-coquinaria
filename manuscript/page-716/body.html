COLD SAUCE FOR BOILED SUCKLING PIG JUS PORRO _[1] FRIGIDUM IN PORCELLUM ELIXUM_

CRUSH PEPPER, CARRAWAY, DILL, LITTLE ORIGANY, PINE NUTS, MOISTEN WITH VINEGAR, BROTH [2], DATE WINE, HONEY, PREPARED MUSTARD; SPRINKLE WITH A LITTLE OIL, PEPPER, AND SERVE.

[1] Tor. only; _porrò_ indicating that the sauce may
also be served with the foregoing. Wanting in List. _et
al._

[2] Wanting in Tor.
